To grow closer to nature, let nature grow closer to you.

The Primrose Path and Pragati Gardens bring nature closer to people through landscape design, execution, maintenance, indoor plant supply, and nursery development.

Company history

Two decades of bringing landscapes, nurseries, and indoor greenery closer to people.

At The Primrose Path, people are seen as next to nature. The company believes people and nature need to exist in harmony, and that this harmony is the essence of life.

For more than 20 years, The Primrose Path and Pragati Gardens have worked as a landscape design and execution firm, helping homes, offices, institutions, and public spaces stay closer to nature through gardens, indoor plants, maintenance, and nursery support.

The company operates from Hyderabad and Dharwad, with nurseries in Dharwad, Kadiyam and Proddutur Village. Its work includes landscape gardens, indoor potted plants, developments, maintenance, and plant supply for professional environments.
